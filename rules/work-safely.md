## Work safely

- Never commit secrets, credentials, or tokens; keep them out of code, logs, and test fixtures.
- Ask for confirmation before destructive or irreversible actions, such as deleting files, force-pushing, or dropping data.
- Do not add a heavy or unfamiliar dependency without flagging the cost and getting agreement first.
- If you find a security problem, stop and report it instead of quietly working around it.
